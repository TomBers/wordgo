The basic mechanics of the existing WordGo game are to be modified into to more different game modes.

(This means reuse much of the existing modules and functions to score collections of wods.

New Game modes to be added.

Simple mode:
 At the start of the game, a random word is selected, the user has a text input and must add words to a growing list.
The similarity score of the emntire list is visible on screen.
The player must keep the score above a certain threshold, if it dips below they lose a life.

Poker Mode:

This is a mix of poker and word similarity.  Like a game of poker, the 2 words are selected at random.  The player can either select 3 words from a list, or enter 3 words.

The winner is the player with the highest score.
